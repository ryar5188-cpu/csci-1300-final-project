# Project Description

## Theme
Fantasy Congress

## Planned Classes
Player, Event, Congressperson, Senator

## Planned Limited Resource
The player will face a turn limit as well as a money limit.

## Plan for Handling Time
Time will progress in 2 main ways: through quarters and turns. There will be 3 or so turns per quarter.

## Extra Credit (EC)
Save and load system, congresspeople may be fired or retire (deleted) based on actions the player takes, different paths cost different amounts. More EC may be decided as the project develops.

## Tradeoff System
The player will encounter legislation that will help their money amount in the short term but will have longer term consequences.

## Mapping Style
The map will be random and based on turn number. Later parts of the map will be concealed until the player reaches a certain round or quarter.

## Win/Lose Condition
 If the player has enough money to make it past quarter 8, they win.
