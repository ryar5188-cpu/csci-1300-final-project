# CSCI 1300 Final Project

## Theme

The player character lives in Washington D.C. and really wants to buy a house, but is very poor. You meet a character named Insider Bill who will let you make money through the three branches of government (and a selection of shady shops) at a price. Bill gives you four quarters with three turns each to do one of the following things per turn: build a roster of Senators and Representatives who will make you money in Congress, buy a pardon from the White House, gamble for money at the Supreme Court, or buy upgrades from Bill or a shopkeeper. It's a devious environment where everything wants something from the player! Can they survive through all of the challenging quarters to buy themselves a house?
